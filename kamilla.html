Dear Kamilla ❤️


Three years ago, our paths timidly crossed. We shared smiles, moments of closeness, but most of all, those invisible sparks that gradually lit up my heart. Then life took us in different directions, as if testing our patience and the depth of our connection. Despite the distance and the silence over all these years, I never truly stopped thinking about you.


Today, our conversations have regained all their intensity, like a fire we thought had died out but only needed a breath to reignite. The sound of your laugh, your words, and even the silences during our calls resonate deeply within me. Each day, I learn a little more about you, and I admire both your strengths and your sensitivity. I cherish this bond between us, and I want to see it flourish, again and again.


Through our messages, our calls, and our shared laughter, I feel a bridge forming between our worlds, and I can’t wait to cross it to meet you, hold you close, and create memories together that go far beyond a screen.


On this Valentine’s Day, I simply wanted to say thank you. Thank you for rekindling that flame by reigniting our conversations, thank you for being here with your tenderness and your joyful spirit, your doubts and your spark. I don’t know what the future holds for us, but I already know you hold a special place in my heart. And for that, I am deeply grateful.


I’m sending you my warmest thoughts on this day of love.

May we soon celebrate these moments free of any screens,

in the warmth of our gazes finally meeting.


Until that day, know that my heart is already yours.

Happy Valentine’s Day, my Valentine.


Your Valentine,

Morgan
